Ruby Gifting is now available! - Kabam's text :

You are now able to buy Rubies and gift them to your Dragons of Atlantis friends and Alliance members.
Not only that, but doing so can get you, the charming benefactor, a Bonus Package!

Here is how it works:
Click on the "Get More Rubies" button under your Rubies balance, then choose the "Gift" link.
From here, you'll be able to select the recipient from either a list of your Dragons of Atlantis

Then, complete your purchase as you would normally.

Once you have completed your purchase, you will receive the Bonus Package associated with the Ruby amount you selected, and the recipient will get the Rubies!
